How LugShare Works

Simple steps to connect travelers and senders worldwide

The LugShare Process

From posting to delivery, here's how our platform works

1

Sign Up & Verify

Create your LugShare account and complete our verification process. Upload your ID, provide contact information, and set up your profile. This ensures trust and safety for all community members.

2

Post or Browse

Travelers: Post your upcoming trips with available luggage space, dates, and destinations.
Senders: Browse available travelers or post your shipping needs with pickup and delivery locations.

3

Connect & Negotiate

Use our secure messaging system to connect with potential matches. Discuss item details, pricing, pickup/delivery arrangements, and any special requirements. Both parties must agree before proceeding.

4

Secure Payment

Complete payment through our secure platform. Funds are held in escrow until successful delivery is confirmed. This protects both senders and travelers throughout the process.

5

Item Pickup

Arrange a convenient pickup location and time. The traveler collects the item from the sender, takes photos for documentation, and confirms receipt through the app.

6

Travel & Updates

The traveler carries your item safely to the destination. Track progress through our app with real-time updates, photos, and location sharing when available.

7

Safe Delivery

The traveler delivers the item to the receiver at the agreed location. Both parties confirm successful delivery through the app, and payment is released to the traveler.

8

Rate & Review

Leave feedback for each other to build trust within the community. Your ratings help other users make informed decisions and maintain our high service standards.
