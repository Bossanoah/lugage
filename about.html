About LugShare

Revolutionizing travel through community-powered luggage sharing

Our Mission

LugShare connects travelers with extra luggage space to people who need to send items worldwide, creating a sustainable, affordable, and community-driven shipping solution.

We believe that travel should be more than just getting from point A to point B. It should be about building connections, helping others, and making the world a smaller, more connected place.

The LugShare Community

Three types of users working together to make shipping affordable and personal

Person A: The Traveler

Frequent flyers and occasional travelers who have extra space in their luggage and want to earn some money while helping others.

Benefits:
  • Earn extra income from unused luggage space
  • Meet interesting people from around the world
  • Make travel more meaningful and purposeful
  • Flexible - choose what items you're comfortable carrying

Person B: The Sender

People who need to send items internationally but want to avoid expensive shipping fees and long delivery times.

Benefits:
  • Save up to 70% on international shipping costs
  • Faster delivery times than traditional shipping
  • Personal touch - real people handling your items
  • Track your items through personal communication

Person C: The Receiver

Recipients who receive items from senders through our trusted traveler network, enjoying personal and reliable delivery.

Benefits:
  • Receive items faster than traditional shipping
  • Personal delivery experience
  • Lower costs passed on from senders
  • Meet travelers and expand your network
